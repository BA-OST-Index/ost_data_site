Illustrator of the Game Development Department at the Millennium School.


Both Midori and her twin sister, Momoi, develop games with the Game Development Department.

Though her introverted personality had previously put her at odds with her more gregarious older sister, the two are now inseparable thanks to their shared love of video games.

Saiba Midori (JP: 才羽 ミドリ) is a 15 years old student from Game Development Department of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Drawing.
