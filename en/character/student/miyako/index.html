Tsukiyuki Miyako

The leader of the RABBIT Squad at SRT Academy. Her call sign is "RABBIT 1."


Miyako originally registered for SRT Academy because of her deep admiration for its adherence to justice. When they suddenly closed down the academy, she was left homeless and camping out in a park with other RABBIT Squad members. She is to-the-point and rarely shows what she's thinking so can come off as uncaring, but has the kindest heart in the squad.


RABBIT 1. Begin the Operation.

“SRT Academy, RABBIT Squad's Tsukiyuki Miyako. Your orders, Sensei.”

Tsukiyuki Miyako (JP: 月雪 ミヤコ) is a 15 years old student from RABBIT Squad of SRT Academy. She enjoys Watching bunny videos.
