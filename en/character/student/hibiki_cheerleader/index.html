A member of the Engineering Department who puts on a cheerleader outfit for game days.


Hibiki's sewing skills are sharp thanks to her love of cosplay, so she designs and makes special event outfits for all the members of the Engineering Department. She made this particular cheerleader outfit a long time ago, so it seems to be a bit small in some places.

“Sensei... Um, uh, wh-what do you think? This is my first time making a cheerleader outfit... It's not too bad, right?”

Nekozuka Hibiki (JP: 猫塚 ヒビキ) is a 15 years old student from Engineering Department of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Cosplay, shopping.
