The faithful head student of Trinity's Sisterhood.


Her sincerity shines through all her actions, making her trusted by many.

She'll take up any duty given to her with piety and serenity. However, when it comes to matters of fashion or everyday life, her traditional nature sometimes causes her trouble.


Through every trial you face, I'll watch over you.

Utazumi Sakurako (JP: 歌住 サクラコ) is a 17 years old student from The Sisterhood of Trinity General School. She enjoys Praying.
