A member of the Abydos Foreclosure Task Force.


A friendly, pleasant girl that provides the Foreclosure Task Force with much-needed emotional support to balance out the extreme personalities of its other members. Though she doesn't show it, she comes from a wealthy family and pays for nearly all of the Foreclosure Task Force's snacks out of her own pocket.


Finally, it's time for me to make my move!

Izayoi Nonomi (JP: 十六夜 ノノミ) is a 16 years old student from Foreclosure Task Force of Abydos High School. She enjoys Shopping.
