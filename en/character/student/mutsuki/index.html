Problem Solver 68's resident go-getter and chief of staff.


Unlike Aru, who has a semblance of a conscience, Mutsuki is a devilish troublemaker who relishes in committing acts of mayhem. As one of her oldest friends, Aru understands her bravado better than anyone, but she doesn't necessarily support it.

Asagi Mutsuki (JP: 浅黄 ムツキ) is a 16 years old student from Problem Solver 68 of Gehenna Academy. She enjoys Collecting bombs.
