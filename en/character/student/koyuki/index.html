A brilliant yet troublesome member of Millennium's Seminar.


She's completely unaware of her unrivaled talent for mathematical decryption. She always sets out to test her luck. Even if she goes too far and lands in hot water, she never stops.


When the plan falls apart, well... That's when life gets interesting!

“Is there anything fun going on? I, Kurosaki Koyuki, am here! Yahahaha!”

Kurosaki Koyuki (JP: 黒崎 コユキ) is a 15 years old student from Seminar of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Testing her luck, escaping.
