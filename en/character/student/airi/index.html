A cheerful member of the Trinity After-School Sweets Club.


Has a relaxed and easy-going personality and cherishes the time she spends sampling desserts and chatting with her friends. Her favorite dessert is ice cream, and she's recently gotten hooked on mint chocolate chip.

Kurimura Airi (JP: 栗村 アイリ) is a 15 years old student from After-School Sweets Club of Trinity General School. She enjoys Checking out dessert shops, tea parties..
