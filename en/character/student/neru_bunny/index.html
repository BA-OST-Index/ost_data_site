The president of C&C who changed into a bunny girl outfit for a special mission.


It's not the kind of outfit Neru would normally wear, but she doesn't hesitate to wear it for a mission.

She is laying low and dialing down her usual temperament for the mission's sake, but she still won't give up her flashy bomber jacket.


Seriously, this is not my cup of tea.

Mikamo Neru (JP: 美甘 ネル) is a 17 years old student from Cleaning & Clearing of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Winning.
