The leader of Filius, one of the allied factions that makes up Trinity General School, and the host of the Tea Party.


Although she has a sweet and friendly demeanor, she rarely reveals her true feelings, so she's not very approachable. Her hobby is preparing tea and refreshments for tea parties. Many Trinity students long to attend her get-togethers.


And how are you? Greetings from Trinity.

Kirifuji Nagisa (JP: 桐藤 ナギサ) is a 17 years old student from Tea Party of Trinity General School. She enjoys Collecting tea leaves, baking pastries, gardening.
