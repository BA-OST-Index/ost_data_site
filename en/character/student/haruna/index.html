President of the Gehenna Gourmet Research Society.


At a glance, she appears to possess the graceful charisma of a refined lady. But when it comes to food, she can turn into a deranged maniac at the flip of a switch. Despite her uncontrollable gluttony, she actually can't stomach too much food at once. Her favorite foods are those rich in fat, such as motsunabe and horumonyaki.


Shall we explore the world of fine dining?

Kurodate Haruna (JP: 黒舘 ハルナ) is a 17 years old student from Gourmet Research Society of Gehenna Academy. She enjoys Foodie touring, making foodie lists.
