A member of Veritas, a hacker group at the Millennium Science School.


A mischievous student who enjoys drawing graffiti and designed Veritas' emblem. She has a flighty personality and doesn't take anything seriously, which often sparks conflict with other clubs.

Konuri Maki (JP: 小塗 マキ) is a 15 years old student from Veritas of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Drawing, playing video games.
