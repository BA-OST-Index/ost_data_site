Iochi Mari

A sincere and benevolent girl affiliated with the Sisterhood.


She always greets Trinity students with a friendly smile. Despite her cuteness, many come to her for counseling because of the calm and relaxing air about her. She is self-critical and always considers herself as lacking, but she dreams of becoming a great Sister like her upperclassmen.

“All encounters are inevitable... It's a pleasure to see you, Sensei.”

Iochi Mari (JP: 伊落 マリー) is a 15 years old student from The Sisterhood of Trinity General School. She enjoys Prayer, meditation.
