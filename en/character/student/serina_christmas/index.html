A member of the Trinity Remedial Knights. She's dressed in a Santa outfit to celebrate Christmas.


She's spending her Christmas volunteering for charity work. Anyone who watches her singing carols and giving out her handmade gifts would think that she was an angel who came down from heaven for Christmas.


The Remedial Knights are here to deliver Christmas cheer! Merry Christmas to all!

Sumi Serina (JP: 鷲見 セリナ) is a 16 years old student from Remedial Knights of Trinity General School. She enjoys Nursing patients, charity work.
