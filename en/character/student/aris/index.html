A mysterious girl who was found sleeping within some ruins. All information regarding Aris' age and origins is unknown. Currently, she has become a hardcore video game fanatic thanks to Midori and Momoi. The majority of her inarticulate speech is comprised of dialog from retro video games.

Tendou Aris (JP: 天童 アリス) is a Age Unknown student from Game Development Department of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Video games (RPGs).
