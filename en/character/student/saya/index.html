A genius inventor of Shanhaijing Academy.


A prodigy who earned six degrees while still in elementary school. However, she's known more for the way she causes trouble for others through her unruly antics than for her genius. She's always accompanied by her rat, Nezusuke, whom she's known since childhood and regards as family.


Leave the experiments to me!

“I'm here! So where's my next test subject?”

Yakushi Saya (JP: 薬子 サヤ) is a 16 years old student from Eastern Alchemy Society of Shanhaijing Academy. She enjoys Research.
