An instructor of the Plum Blossom Garden who drank Saya's elixir and transformed into her smaller self.


Even though her body is young, her mind is the same, so she tries to speak with maturity, but she sometimes gives in to her childlike instincts.


It's really convenient being this small again!

“Don't treat me like a child! I'm still everyone's favorite older sister at heart!”

Sunohara Shun (JP: 春原 シュン) is a Top Secret student from Plum Blossom Garden of Shanhaijing Academy. She enjoys Eating sweets.
