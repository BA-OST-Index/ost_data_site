The sporty field captain of the Abydos Foreclosure Task Force.


A girl of few words and almost no facial expression, Shiroko gives the impression of being frigid and emotionless. In truth, she is more devoted to the Abydos High School than anything else. If it's for the sake of revitalizing the academy, she'll do whatever it takes and sometimes propose some outlandish ideas herself.


Do you need my help?

Sunaookami Shiroko (JP: 砂狼 シロコ) is a 16 years old student from Foreclosure Task Force of Abydos High School. She enjoys Jogging, cycling.
