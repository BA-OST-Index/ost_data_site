A friendly, outgoing member of the Trinity Make-Up Work Club.


Hifumi's just a regular girl with average grades, but she's nonetheless well liked and popular for her well-rounded personality. She's a good listener for anyone who needs to vent about their worries or problems, but this also causes her to get swept up in the moment and unintentionally cause trouble.


If Sensei's going to be with me...I'm going to give my all!

Ajitani Hifumi (JP: 阿慈谷 ヒフミ) is a 16 years old student from Make-Up Work Club of Trinity General School. She enjoys Collecting Peroro merch, shopping, listening to others.
