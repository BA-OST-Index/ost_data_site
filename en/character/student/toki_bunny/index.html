A dedicated C&C agent, Toki changed into a bunny costume to better camouflage her in infiltration operations.


Her specialty under Rio was never infiltrations, so she's a little nervous about these new types of missions. But regardless, she's excited to finally follow in the footsteps of her C&C upperclassmen, no matter how much she tries to hide it.


I am ready to commence operation at anytime. ...Is there a problem with my outfit?

“Hello, Sensei. It's me, Toki. Prepare to see the true power of an undercover agent.”

Asuma Toki (JP: 飛鳥馬 トキ) is a 16 years old student from Cleaning & Clearing of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Tracking.
