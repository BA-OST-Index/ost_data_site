Junko is wearing a kimono for the new year.


She went through the trouble of putting on a new outfit, and has been looking forward to New Year's food. Once she starts eating, she won't tolerate interruption.

“Happy New Year, Sensei! Let's go find something delicious!”

Akashi Junko (JP: 赤司 ジュンコ) is a 15 years old student from Gourmet Research Society of Gehenna Academy. She enjoys Food touring.
