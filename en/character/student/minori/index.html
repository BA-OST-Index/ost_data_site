Head of Red Winter Federal Academy's Labor Party.


She dispatches and manages laborers wherever they are needed, including large-scale construction sites. Despite her ability to get any job done, she has a bad reputation with employers due to being prone to leading strikes and protests at the slightest hint of trouble.


All the pudding in Kivotos for the students

“Civil engineering, construction, demolition... Contact us if you need workers. We'll even agitate for you!”

Yasumori Minori (JP: 安守 ミノリ) is a 17 years old student from Labor Party of Red Winter Federal Academy. She enjoys Strikes, Protests, Demonstrations.
