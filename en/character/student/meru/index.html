A member of the Knowledge Liberation Front, a book club at Red Winter Academy.


Unlike her club mate Momiji, she doesn't just read fan books—she creates them. Writing under the name "Meruri," she draws inspiration from the drama between her fellow students. Even the feud between the Trinity and Gehenna student bodies have made it into her pages, generating some notoriety outside of Red Winter Academy.


Get up... It's time...to draw...

“Hallo! I'm Himeki Meru from the Knowledge Liberation Front! But you can call me by my pen name, Meruri. Nice to meet you, Sensei.”

Himeki Meru (JP: 姫木 メル) is a 16 years old student from Knowledge Liberation Front of Red Winter Federal Academy. She enjoys Drawing comics, fantasizing.
