A devoted Justice Task Force member who escorted Tsurugi to the beach.


Mashiro does not stop training while other students go out to play and make summer memories. She believes that this is the way of justice.


Justice doesn't back down, even in sweltering heat!

“Trinity Justice Task Force first-year, Mashiro. Wherever it may be, under any circumstance, I will support you, Sensei—with all my might.”

Shizuyama Mashiro (JP: 静山 マシロ) is a 15 years old student from Justice Task Force of Trinity General School. She enjoys Taking high ground and notes.
