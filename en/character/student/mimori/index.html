Hyakkiyako Academy Inner Discipline Club's kind vice president.


A popular girl because she always has a gentle smile and is kind to everyone. The childhood friend of Tsubaki, the president of the Inner Discipline Club. She joined the club to fulfill her dream of becoming an elegant lady like the ones in the shoujo manga she used to read when she was young. She continues to train diligently by cooking and cleaning to make her dream come true.


I still have a long way to go...

but I'll show you the results of my training.

“Heehee. Hello, Sensei. I'm Mimori from the Inner Discipline Club. I look forward to working with you.”

Mizuha Mimori (JP: 水羽 ミモリ) is a 16 years old student from Inner Discipline Club of Allied Hyakkiyako Academy. She enjoys Reading shoujo manga, homemaking.
