Takanashi Hoshino

Related Characters
Variants Hoshino (Swimsuit)
Other Characters The Black Suit

The Abydos Student Council vice president and president of the Foreclosure Task Force.


Acts more like a tired old timer and would rather play pranks than do actual work. This often gets her into trouble with her fellow task force members, but once on a mission, she fights harder on the front lines than anyone else to keep her friends safe.


It's way past my nap time,

but I'll help if you want me to.

Takanashi Hoshino (JP: 小鳥遊 ホシノ) is a 17 years old student from Foreclosure Task Force of Abydos High School. She enjoys Taking naps, procrastinating.
