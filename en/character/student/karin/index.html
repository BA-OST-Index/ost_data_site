An agent of C&C, a secret organization from the Millennium Science School.


She's in charge of providing rear fire support under the call sign Zero-Two. Despite her intimidating appearance, she is the most cautious of C&C's agents and often ends up having to reel in Asuna and Akane when they get out of control during missions.


As you wish, Master.

Kakudate Karin (JP: 角楯 カリン) is a 16 years old student from Cleaning & Clearing of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Cleaning.
