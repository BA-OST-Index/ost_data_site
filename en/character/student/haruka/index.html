A senior member of Problem Solver 68.


Her gloomy personality has made her the target of bullies since childhood. Only recently was Aru able to help her put a stop to the harassment. Since then, she has been the youngest senior member of Problem Solver 68. She may be timid and have painfully low self-esteem, but may also be the most terrifying member of her club because of her ideas alone.

Igusa Haruka (JP: 伊草 ハルカ) is a 15 years old student from Problem Solver 68 of Gehenna Academy. She enjoys Weeding.
