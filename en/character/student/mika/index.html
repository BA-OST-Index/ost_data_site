The former leader of Pater, one of the allied factions that makes up Trinity General School.


A former Tea Party member and a childhood friend of Nagisa, another member of the Tea Party. When the two clash politically, she draws a clear line between their public disagreements and their private friendship.

She seems lively and cheerful, and she's never without a smile. However, her demeanor hides worries that she can't reveal, making her something of an enigma.


Thanks for waiting! It's my turn to jump in! ☆

Misono Mika (JP: 聖園 ミカ) is a 17 years old student from Tea Party of Trinity General School. She enjoys Chatting, collecting accessories.
