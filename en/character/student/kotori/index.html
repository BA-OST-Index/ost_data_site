Member of the Engineering Department at the Millennium Science School.


A chatterbox who loves discussing topics such as physics and mechanics. She's always the first one at the scene to explain what's happening whenever there's a machinery-related problem at Millennium.

Toyomi Kotori (JP: 豊見 コトリ) is a 15 years old student from Engineering Department of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Talking.
