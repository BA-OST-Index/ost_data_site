An enthusiastic member of the Hyakkiyako Academy Inner Discipline Club.


She used to be infamous for spreading mischief around town, but after meeting Tsubaki and Mimori by chance, she was inspired to be a proper lady like them and joined the Inner Discipline Club. Kaede never skips her training to be a proper lady, but still always seems to cause trouble.


As usual, I'm gonna work hard to become a proper lady!

“Ta-da! The mascot of the Inner Discipline Club is here! The wait is over, Sensei! What should we do today?”

Isami Kaede (JP: 勇美 カエデ) is a 15 years old student from Inner Discipline Club of Allied Hyakkiyako Academy. She enjoys Playing pranks (but is trying to be good lately), catching beetles.
