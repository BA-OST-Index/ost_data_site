A member of the School Lunch Club that manages the Gehenna Academy's dining hall.


Like Fuuka, she's a hard worker who pours her heart and soul into cooking meals for the other students. However, her cooking is so bad that it's grotesque, so her efforts are ultimately useless. But she continues to strive to hopefully improve her cooking under Fuuka's encouragement and guidance.

Ushimaki Juri (JP: 牛牧 ジュリ) is a 15 years old student from School Lunch Club of Gehenna Academy. She enjoys Culinary research.
