The head of the Medical Committee who happens to visit the hot springs resort while on duty.


Chinatsu's problems don't stop even at the snowy hot springs resort—from ordering new medicine to the latest Hot Springs Development Department conspiracy. However, the scenery of the idyllic hot springs makes the weary Chinatsu a bit indolent.


I want to take it...slow today.

“Overworking can backfire. How about relaxing today and enjoying a break with me?”

Hinomiya Chinatsu (JP: 火宮 チナツ) is a 15 years old student from Prefect Team of Gehenna Academy. She enjoys Reading philosophy books.
