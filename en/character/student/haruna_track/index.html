Haruna has changed into her gym clothes for the Kivotos Halo Festival.


No matter where she is, Haruna's quest for food never ends. This time, she's set her eyes on the culinary delights that can only be found at Kivotos' biggest athletic event. Maybe it's because of the excitement in the air, but she seems even more friendly than usual.


The more you move, the more appetite you'll work up. Shall we?

“The season of exercise...and appetite! There's a culinary feast to be enjoyed...and I have no choice but to take advantage of this opportunity.”

Kurodate Haruna (JP: 黒舘 ハルナ) is a 17 years old student from Gourmet Research Society of Gehenna Academy. She enjoys Taste testing, gourmet research.
