A member of the Hyakkiyako Academy's Ninjutsu Research Club.


A bright and bubbly young girl, but her devotion to her strange dream has caused her to spend most of her life as a loner. She devotes herself to becoming the best ninja she can every day. That also includes following around and referring to Sensei as her lord among other things.


Anything for my lord!

Kuda Izuna (JP: 久田 イズナ) is a 15 years old student from Ninjutsu Research Club of Allied Hyakkiyako Academy. She enjoys Studying the art of ninjutsu, squiring her lord.
