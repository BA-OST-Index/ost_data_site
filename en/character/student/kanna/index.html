A police student in charge of the Public Peace Bureau at the Valkyrie Police School.


She's better known by the nickname "Feral Hound," because she never gives up on a crime once it's in her sights. She'll investigate tenaciously until she uncovers the truth. People see her as hot-tempered because of her unusual, ferocious looks and her nickname, but in reality she's calm and down-to-earth.


I will serve the citizens faithfully.

Ogata Kanna (JP: 尾刃 カンナ) is a 17 years old student from Public Peace Bureau of Valkyrie Police School. She enjoys Reading detective stories, watching detective movies.
