The vice president of the Inner Discipline Club has changed into a swimsuit for the summer.


She's known for her gentle smile and kind demeanor, but dressing in swimwear has her a little more excited than usual.


If only we could freeze this moment in time...

“The summer... The beach... It's all so blissful, Sensei. Of course, I haven't forgotten about my training.”

Mizuha Mimori (JP: 水羽 ミモリ) is a 16 years old student from Inner Discipline Club of Allied Hyakkiyako Academy. She enjoys Reading shoujo manga, homemaking.
