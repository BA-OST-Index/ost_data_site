A laid-back administrative advisor of Valkyrie Police School.


She entered Valkyrie longing for a laid-back, easygoing life as a civil servant. She remains negligent, enjoying life as it is without being swayed by money or fame even when a case is sent to the Public Safety Bureau. Her favorite activity is eating donuts while listening to the radio.

Nemugaki Fubuki (JP: 合歓垣 フブキ) is a 15 years old student from Public Safety Bureau of Valkyrie Police School. She enjoys Crosswords, radio listening.
