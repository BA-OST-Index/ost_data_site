Scenario writer of the Game Development Department at the Millennium School.


Both Momoi and her twin sister, Midori, develop games with the Game Development Department. Though her lighthearted and cheerful personality previously put her at odds with her more introverted little sister, the two are now inseparable thanks to their shared love of video games.


Saiba Momoi (JP: 才羽 モモイ) is a 15 years old student from Game Development Department of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Video Games.
