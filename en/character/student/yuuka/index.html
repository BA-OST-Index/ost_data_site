The treasurer of Seminar, the student council of the Millennium Science School.


Yuuka has a mathematical acumen that is notable even among Millennium's high ratio of STEM students. She oversees the entirety of Millennium's budget. Her special talent is the abacus, and she has a habit of fiddling with the beads when dealing with emotional or mental conflicts.

Hayase Yuuka (JP: 早瀬 ユウカ) is a 16 years old student from Seminar of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Doing calculations.
