Story/[E2] 無法計算的價值

無法計算的價值
General Information
Story Name 無法計算的價值
Story Position Yuuka
Bond Story 2
Special Case
Battle Included No
Recollection Lobby No

無法計算的價值 is the 2nd story of the bond story of the student Yuuka.

Descriptions:

  • 優香從欠千年學園債款的企業家收到昂貴的畫作為擔保。為了看懂那幅看起來像塗鴉的抽象畫的價值，她傷腦筋許久。在老師的指點下才發現那幅抽象畫是假的。

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.
