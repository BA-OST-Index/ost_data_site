Responsible Spending is the 1st story of the bond story of the student Yuuka.

Descriptions:

  • When Yuuka dropped by Schale's office to hand in a report, she learned of Sensei's disorganized and carefree lifestyle. In response, Yuuka scolds Sensei and organizes the receipts in the office.

Battle/Bond Track Info

  • OST  17. Irasshaimase

Story Parts

1. [Momotalk] About The Bill

Yuuka got in touch with Sensei and said that she would like to count the weapon usage in the previous battle to generate a financial report. Sensei said that he didn't know how to fill a said report and Yuuka promised that she would assist Sensei the next time she dropped by Schale.

2. Visiting Schale

Yuuka visited Schale while Sensei was having lunch, and so she started looking over Sensei's bills. Soon, Yuuka noticed Sensei's account balance was quite unusual and found that Sensei had multiple large purchases. And for this reason Yuuka started checking all the bills and invoices.
