Story/[E3] 人體計算機

人體計算機
General Information
Story Name 人體計算機
Story Position Yuuka
Bond Story 3
Special Case
Battle Included No
Recollection Lobby No

人體計算機 is the 3rd story of the bond story of the student Yuuka.

Descriptions:

  • 老師從不良學生手中搶來備用品，但其單位制卻和夏萊的備用品不同，所以正為此事傷腦筋。優香見狀便發揮快速的計算能力幫助老師。

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.
