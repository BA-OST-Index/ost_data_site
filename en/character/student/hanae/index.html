A cheerful and energetic first-year member of the Trinity Remedial Knights.


Hanae has a vivacious personality and a positive outlook on life, but when she sees a patient, she can get too worked up and often actually worsens their condition. She's a bit odd at times, but patients under her care seem to recover more quickly regardless of their affliction.

“Your Ministering Angel has arrived!”

Asagao Hanae (JP: 朝顔 ハナエ) is a 15 years old student from Remedial Knights of Trinity General School. She enjoys Dancing, cheerleading.
