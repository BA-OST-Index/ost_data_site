A member of the Vigilante Crew responsible for protecting Trinity's students.


To combat the increasing number of attacks on Trinity's students at the hands of the other academies, Suzumi volunteered to patrol the streets to abate attackers. Often misunderstood as cold and unfeeling due to her strong sense of justice, she actually has a friendly side as well.

“Does this count as a part of the Vigilante Crew's duties? I'll do what's necessary to uphold Trinity's peace.”

Morizuki Suzumi (JP: 守月 スズミ) is a 16 years old student from Trinity's Vigilante Crew of Trinity General School. She enjoys Going on patrols and walks.
