A member of the Trinity After-School Sweets Club, head troublemaker, and a self-proclaimed romantic.


Has a habit of being strangely philosophical and comparing everything to desserts, making people around her feel awkward. She claims she simply wants to share her romanticism with everyone. However, it's impossible to deny that Natsu may just be messing with everyone since her definition of "romance" is always changing.


Have you ever thought about shortcakes, Sensei?

“Hey, Sensei. I hope your day is sweet like candy!”

Yutori Natsu (JP: 柚鳥 ナツ) is a 15 years old student from After-School Sweets Club of Trinity General School. She enjoys Romanticizing, meditation.
