Secretary for the Millennium School Student Council, Seminar.


Her main responsibility is to maintain Seminar's official records, but she also handles various other legal and administrative duties, such as overseeing patent applications and product evaluations for Millennium student creations. Her extraordinary memory allows her

to almost perfectly remember anything she's read or heard once.


Will you come away with me to the realm of reason?

“I am Ushio Noa, Seminar's secretary. I look forward to your support, Sensei.”

Ushio Noa (JP: 生塩 ノア) is a 16 years old student from Seminar of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Reading, recitation.
