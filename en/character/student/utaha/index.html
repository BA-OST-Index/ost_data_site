President of the Engineering Department at the Millennium Science School.


Has invented a variety of robots, if only to stake her claim to the title of president of the Engineering Department. Her pet robot Thundergun has been determined to possess combat capabilities equivalent to a hundred PMC combat automata.

Shiraishi Utaha (JP: 白石 ウタハ) is a 17 years old student from Engineering Department of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Inventing, making repairs.
