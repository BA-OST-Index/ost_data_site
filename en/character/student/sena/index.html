President of Gehenna's always busy Medical Emergency Club.


She transports the wounded with a stoic face no matter the situation. She may seem apathetic as she treats the wounded like inanimate cargo, but she has never once complained about transporting numerous injured people all over Gehenna.


Where is the corpse...? I mean, where is the injured patient?

Himuro Sena (JP: 氷室 セナ) is a 17 years old student from Medical Emergency Club of Gehenna Academy. She enjoys Being on standby, organizing medicine.
