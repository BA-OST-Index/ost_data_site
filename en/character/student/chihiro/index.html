Vice president of Veritas, Millennium Science School's hacker group.


A genius programmer with excellent hacking skills, she is not afraid of getting her hands dirty when the task demands, whether it's going undercover or in the field. She actually has common sense unlike most of Veritas' devilish members. She is always emphasizing proper hacking ethics to other members.

Kagami Chihiro (JP: 各務 チヒロ) is a 17 years old student from Veritas of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Shopping for electronics.
