The secretary of the Abydos Foreclosure Task Force who flew the group to the beach for summer vacation.

She wants to forget about her arduous daily grind and have a pleasant vacation, but she can never hide her strong work ethic, even while relaxing.

“I-I'm Okusora Ayane. H-How do I look? I feel kind of embarrassed.”

Okusora Ayane (JP: 奥空 アヤネ) is a 15 years old student from Foreclosure Task Force of Abydos High School. She enjoys Balancing her checkbook, collecting antiques.
