An engineer of Veritas, the hacker group of the Millennium Science School.


An unprecedented genius even amongst the brilliant minds at Millennium. Most of the school's cutting-edge technology was developed by her. Even with all these achievements, she remains humble, friendly, and considerate towards her peers.

Omagari Hare (JP: 小鈎 ハレ) is a 16 years old student from Veritas of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Video games, watching movies.
