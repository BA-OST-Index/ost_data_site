An Arius Squad member that gives a gloomy impression.


Hiyori follows other Arius students around, always crying about her latest heavy burden. She has low self-esteem and a negative personality. She tends to be pessimistic about everything in the world, and believes the only purpose of life is suffering.


Oh, I got lucky today. Heehee...

“Heehee... Finally, I made it. It's gonna be really hard, right? But I've learned that's just life... I'm Hiyori. Nice to meet you...”

Tsuchinaga Hiyori (JP: 槌永 ヒヨリ) is a 16 years old student from Arius Squad of Arius Satellite School. She enjoys Collecting magazines.
