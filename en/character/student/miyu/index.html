The sniper of the SRT Academy RABBIT Squad. Her call sign is "RABBIT 4."


Miyu entered the SRT Academy due to her natural sniping talent, but struggled with her meek and shy temperament. Just as she found a home in the academy, it closed down and she joined the rest of her squad camping out in the park. Her lack of presence makes her a great sniper, but sometimes even her allies forget she exists.


Th-This is RABBIT 4. D-Did you call...?

Kasumizawa Miyu (JP: 霞沢 ミユ) is a 15 years old student from RABBIT Squad of SRT Special Academy. She enjoys Pessimism, escapism, collecting pebbles.
