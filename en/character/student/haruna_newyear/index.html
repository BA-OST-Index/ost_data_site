The president of the Gourmet Research Society, who got dressed up in a kimono for the New Year.


She believes all aspects of a situation are crucial for a gourmand: not just the time and place of the meal, but also wearing the appropriate outfit. Therefore, she has set out on the path of epicureanism wearing a kimono. She certainly seems elegant and refined, but...if she's served food that doesn't meet her standards, watch out.


What's more appetizing than seasonal food? For the New Year, we could have osechi, ozoni, oshiruko... Oh, now I'm hungry.

Kurodate Haruna (JP: 黒舘 ハルナ) is a 17 years old student from Gourmet Research Society of Gehenna Academy. She enjoys Taste testing, gourmet research.
