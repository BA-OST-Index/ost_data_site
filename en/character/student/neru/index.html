President of C&C, a secret organization of the Millennium School.


At first glance, Neru looks like nothing but a young delinquent with a flashy bomber jacket draped over her maid outfit, but in reality she's Millennium's most formidable agent. Within Millennium, her call sign, Double O, is seen as a sign of certain victory.


Shout! Give it everything you've got!

Mikamo Neru (JP: 美甘 ネル) is a 17 years old student from Cleaning & Clearing of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Winning.
