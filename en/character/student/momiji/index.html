A literature enthusiast fighting to preserve knowledge in Red Winter Academy's library.


Most of her peers fight for bread and snacks, but she fights for books. Unfortunately, she's a serious otaku, which means that the Secrateriat rejects most of her requests to get more manga in the library... But does Momiji give up? Never!

“I'm Akiizumi Momiji from the Red Winter Federal Academy Knowledge Liberation Front. Let's work together to protect all the world's knowledge!”

Akiizumi Momiji (JP: 秋泉 モミジ) is a 15 years old student from Knowledge Liberation Front of Red Winter Federal Academy. She enjoys Collecting manga.
