Executive Officer of Genryumon, Shanhaijing's student council, and the Student Council President's personal bodyguard.


Despite her calm and collected demeanor, she's a hardcore classic noir movie buff. She likes to quote scenes and lines from movies, although often incorrectly. She gets into trouble trying to be cool at inappropriate times, but Genryumon members do their best to mitigate the damage.


One must fight their own battles.

No good comes from involving others.

Konoe Mina (JP: 近衛 ミナ) is a 16 years old student from Genryumon of Shanhaijing Academy. She enjoys Watching movies (hardboiled ones!).
