The backbone of the Hot Springs Department and foreman at their construction sites.


She believes with all her heart that demolition can solve any problem.

Her personality is bold and confident, and she's earned the trust of everyone in the Hot Springs Department due to her excellent demolition skills.


Is there a problem? Let me handle it!

“Yay! What do you want me to work on? Where should I dig? I can excavate whatever you want!”

Shimokura Megu (JP: 下倉 メグ) is a 17 years old student from Hot Springs Department of Gehenna Academy. She enjoys Demolition.
