Chair of the Security Committee in charge of Red Winter Academy's safety.


Though she's known as cold-hearted disciplinarian, the truth is she blindly follows President Cherino's orders without thinking. She likes the word "charge" and is the first to run off when a problem occurs within the Red Winter Academy, but often loses her way due to her terrible sense of direction.


Running away doesn't help one bit!

Ikekura Marina (JP: 池倉 マリナ) is a 16 years old student from Red Winter Office of Red Winter Federal Academy. She enjoys Marching, Charging.
