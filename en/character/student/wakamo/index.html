One of the Seven Prisoners that escaped the Corrections Bureau after being suspended from Allied Hyakkiyako Academy.


She is the feared Fox of Calamity due to her destructive ways. Her motives are a mystery, and there are several rumors surrounding the face under her mask. Some say there's a monstrous devil underneath, but it remains only a rumor.


Oh... We meet at last. ♥

“Heeheehee. Finally found you. I won't lose you again. Please don't leave my sight. ♥”

Kosaka Wakamo (JP: 狐坂 ワカモ) is a 18 years old student from None of Allied Hyakkiyako Academy. She enjoys Destroying, looting.
