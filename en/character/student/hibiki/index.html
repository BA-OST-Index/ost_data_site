Member of the Engineering Department at Millennium Science School.


Socially inept and inarticulate compared to other students, but is a prolific inventor due to her innate talent for engineering. Her inventions are considered to be flawless masterpieces, but they often come equipped with baffling and obscure functions.


Have anything that needs fixing?

“I've been waiting for this and knew it would come. The moment when you would call my name.”

Nekozuka Hibiki (JP: 猫塚 ヒビキ) is a 15 years old student from Engineering Department of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Cosplay, shopping.
