The Foreclosure Task Force president who flew to an uninhabited island to relax for summer. She always says she wants to sit around and do nothing, so she's perfectly prepared for summer. She's unaware that she looks a little more excited than usual.


Come here, Sensei. It's really cool here.

Takanashi Hoshino (JP: 小鳥遊 ホシノ) is a 17 years old student from Foreclosure Task Force of Abydos High School. She enjoys Taking naps, procrastinating.
