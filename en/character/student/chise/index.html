A spacey girl who is a member of the Hyakkiyako Academy's Yin-Yang Club.


Extremely fond of traditional activities such as writing haiku, which led her to join the Yin-Yang Club. Her exotic looks and presence have made her the envy of her peers at Hyakkiyako, but she seems completely oblivious to that fact.

“Um... So, what should we do now?”

Waraku Chise (JP: 和楽 チセ) is a 16 years old student from Yin-Yang Club of Allied Hyakkiyako Academy. She enjoys Writing haiku.
