A member of the Trinity After-School Sweets Club.


A girl with a height complex that gives off the impression of a small, angry animal. Constantly fixated on appearing more mature, her tendency to wear her heart on her sleeve gets her into trouble. She can't help but smile widely when visiting dessert cafes to sample the latest limited-edition treats.

Ibaragi Yoshimi (JP: 伊原木 ヨシミ) is a 15 years old student from After-School Sweets Club of Trinity General School. She enjoys Participating in events, collecting limited-edition desserts.
