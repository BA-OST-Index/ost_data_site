A hardworking and kindhearted girl affiliated with the Sisterhood.


In charge of caring for the Trinity Cathedral as she completes duties and remains polite to all. However, she often makes mistakes thanks to her clumsy disposition and lack of confidence. She always carries an installable grenade machine gun in her bag, but never seems to struggle with its weight.


Do you need help with something?

“I-I'm Wakaba Hinata. Nice to meet you.”

Wakaba Hinata (JP: 若葉 ヒナタ) is a 17 years old student from The Sisterhood of Trinity General School. She enjoys Praying, cleaning.
