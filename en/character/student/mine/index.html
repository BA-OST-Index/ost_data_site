The captain of the Trinity Remedial Knights. A true knight in shining armor, she's upright and resolute, but she has a radical side as well.


She has a reputation in Trinity: Mine destroys things, and her knights pick up the pieces. She spends her spare time maintaining her equipment and making snacks and drinks for get-togethers with her friends. Some say that she knows as much about black tea as Hasumi of the Justice Task Force.


I'm Aomori Mine, Remedial Knights captain. Does anyone need medical assistance?

Aomori Mine (JP: 蒼森 ミネ) is a 17 years old student from Remedial Knights of Trinity General School. She enjoys Collecting medical supplies.
