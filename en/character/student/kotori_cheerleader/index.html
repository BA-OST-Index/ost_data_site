This member of the Engineering Department donned a cheerleading outfit to support her friends.


Her cheers are loud, upbeat, and—most importantly—filled with technical explanations.


Knowledge is always useful, no matter where you are!

“Whether you need keen scientific insight or a spirited cheer, just leave it to me!”

Toyomi Kotori (JP: 豊見 コトリ) is a 15 years old student from Engineering Department of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Talking.
