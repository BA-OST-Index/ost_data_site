A cold-blooded specialist of Gehenna's Prefect Team participating in summer boot camp.


Closely monitored Gehenna's troublemakers from getting out of line at the beach alongside other Prefect Team members. She's deeply disappointed that summer camp was ruined by the constant appearance of those fools, even if she tries to hide it.


There had better not be any fools caught up in breaking the rules.

“I'm not joking! This is all part of the recon mission.”

Shiromi Iori (JP: 銀鏡 イオリ) is a 16 years old student from Prefect Team of Gehenna Academy. She enjoys Going on patrol, castigating others.
