A member of the Gehenna Gourmet Research Society with a voracious appetite.


She loves food so much that she even gobbles up dishes that others would dislike with gusto.


The world is full of delicious food!

Shishidou Izumi (JP: 獅子堂 イズミ) is a 16 years old student from Gourmet Research Society of Gehenna Academy. She enjoys Cooking and eating bizarre dishes.
