The eternal boss of Problem Solver 68.


Aru spends a lot of money on getting her hair done and renting an outfit to wear to the New Year's event in the hopes that her fixer agency will prosper in the new year.Perhaps because she feels more confident than usual, she has been putting on the air of a dignified boss, walking and talking in a nonchalant and carefree way.


Heeheehee. This is the first commission of the year, right?

Okay, I'll give it my best.

“Heehee. Can I still prove useful to you this year? There's no other choice. Stay by my side and watch what I accomplish this year.”

Rikuhachima Aru (JP: 陸八魔 アル) is a 16 years old student from Problem Solver 68 of Gehenna Academy. She enjoys Studying management.
