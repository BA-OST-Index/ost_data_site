Murokasa Akane


General Information
Full Name Murokasa Akane
室笠 アカネ
Age 16 years old
Birthday April 1st
School/Club Info Millennium Science School
Cleaning & Clearing
Hobby Cleaning
Related Characters
Variants Akane (Bunny)
Other Characters Nothing to show!

An agent of C&C, a secret organization of the Millennium School. Akane operates under the call sign Zero-Three. She became known as the Master Cleaner for her modus operandi of infiltrating the enemy camp and clearing it out with explosives, all while wearing her signature gentle smile.

“My fondest greetings to you, Master. I pledge to give you only the best service.”

Murokasa Akane (JP: 室笠 アカネ) is a 16 years old student from Cleaning & Clearing of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Cleaning.
