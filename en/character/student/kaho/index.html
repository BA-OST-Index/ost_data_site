Vice president of the Hyakkiyako Academy and the Lead Strategist for the Tourism and Culture Public Relations Support Team.


A member of the Yin-Yang Club, she is in charge of Hyakkiyako's public relations and marketing. So, naturally, she's an avid fan of all things Hyakkiyako and cares deeply about the students who work together to build the culture she loves. Some students even whisper that Kaho feels more like the president than their actual president. In a pool of eccentric members, she's the only one with a healthy amount of common sense in the Yin-Yang Club and that alone makes her an invaluable part of the team.


Allow me to show you Hyakkiyako's impeccable charm, Sensei.

“My name is Kuwakami Kaho, the vice president of Allied Hyakkiyako Academy's Yin-Yang Club. I'm pleased to make your acquaintance.”

Kuwakami Kaho (JP: 桑上 カホ) is a 17 years old student from Yin-Yang Club of Allied Hyakkiyako Academy. She enjoys Promoting Hyakkiyako's culture, collecting Hyakkiyako merch.
