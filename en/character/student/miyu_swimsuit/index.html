The RABBIT Squad's sniper, who's visiting a remote fishing village on an undercover operation.


She brought her fishing tackle to blend in and spends most of her time at the docks. Occasionally, she catches an unexpected big fish and attracts the attention of other anglers, but...her natural lack of presence remains strong even in this peculiar village.

Kasumizawa Miyu (JP: 霞沢 ミユ) is a 15 years old student from RABBIT Squad of SRT Academy. She enjoys Pessimism, escapism, collecting pebbles.
