An agent of the Super Phenomenon Task Force at the Millennium School.


An emotionally inscrutable oddball, Eimi very rarely speaks and can often be found staring off into space for no particular reason. However, there's no one as brutally efficient as her when it comes to carrying out missions for Seminar.

Izumimoto Eimi (JP: 和泉元 エイミ) is a 15 years old student from Super Phenomenon Task Force of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Spacing out, listening to music.
