A devoted member of Trinity General School's Justice Task Force.


Armed with a massive sniper rifle that towers over her, Mashiro stoically provides fire support.Though not much one for words, she isn't shy or socially inept and actually becomes quite talkative when it comes to justice.


Where do you need justice dispensed?

“I'm Mashiro, first year from the Justice Task Force. I may be inexperienced, but I'm here to help however I can.”

Shizuyama Mashiro (JP: 静山 マシロ) is a 15 years old student from Justice Task Force of Trinity General School. She enjoys Climbing to high places, keeping an observational diary.
