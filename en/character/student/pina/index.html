A devout vigilante admirer attending Hyakkiyako Academy.


Pina has a bubbly, energetic personality and takes pleasure in helping others. Everything she knows about vigilantes comes from movies, so the accuracy of said knowledge can be somewhat dubious. Nonetheless, she persists in her ambition of becoming a vigilante herself.

Asahina Pina (JP: 朝比奈 フィーナ) is a 17 years old student from Festival Operations Department of Allied Hyakkiyako Academy. She enjoys Watching vigilante movies.
