The chief secretary of the Red Winter Office and President Cherino's closest aide.


A speaking master who can wrap up and deliver just about any message. She gives regular speeches at the academy to inspire loyalty to President Cherino. Cherino believes Tomoe is loyal because she is smitten by her charisma, but in fact, Tomoe simply finds Cherino adorable.

“Nice to meet you, Sensei. Are you here to learn about the accomplishments of the great President Cherino?”

Sashiro Tomoe (JP: 佐城 トモエ) is a 17 years old student from Red Winter Office of Red Winter Federal Academy. She enjoys Rallying.
