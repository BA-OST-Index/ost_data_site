A girl from the Hyakkiyako Ninjutsu Research Club.


Tsukuyo is insecure about her towering height, as she was teased relentlessly for it as a child. She is timid and has a hard time speaking up, but also determinedly follows her goals once she's made a decision. While she doesn't know much about ninjas, she does her best for Michiru and Izuna so she won't let them down.


I-I'm just a tall, good-for-nothing... But I want to do my best to help, Sensei!

“Hello, I'm Ono Tsukuyo... I'm a member of the Ninjutsu Research Club... Nice to meet you...”

Ono Tsukuyo (JP: 大野 ツクヨ) is a 15 years old student from Ninjutsu Research Club of Allied Hyakkiyako Academy. She enjoys Disguising as a tree, hiding.
