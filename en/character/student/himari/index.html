Akeboshi Himari


General Information

The former president of Veritas, a hacker club at the Millennium School, and the current president of the Super Phenomenon Task Force.


She styles herself as a super genius hacker, and claims she has a degree in Omniscience, which only three people have received in Millennium's history. However, no one's ever been able to confirm the existence of such a degree. Himari relishes this, believing that secrets make a girl more beautiful. None can deny that she is an extremely talented hacker with a strange taste for the occult.


Haha! Is it finally time to shine for Millennium's super genius hacker?

“Allow me to introduce myself. I am the president of Veritas and of the Super Phenomenon Task Force, the delicately chic and beautiful super genius hacker of Millennium, the one and only Akeboshi Himari! Try to contain your excitement, Sensei.”

Akeboshi Himari (JP: 明星 ヒマリ) is a 17 years old student from Super Phenomenon Task Force of Millennium Science School. She enjoys Astrology, the occult.
