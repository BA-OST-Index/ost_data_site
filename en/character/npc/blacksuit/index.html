The Black Suit
General Information
Full Name The Black Suit (黒服)
Related Characters
Other Characters Hoshino

The Black Suit (黒服) is an NPC in the game.

Description

The Black Suit is a villain in Blue Archive, a member of Gematria. His main goal is to investigate the "Mythic" in Kivotos, as well as exploit the power of the AI so-called Decagrammaton. He appears in multiple stories.
