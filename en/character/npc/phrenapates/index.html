Phrenapates (プレナパテス) is an NPC in the game.

Description

Phrenapates is an adult appeared in the Volume Final. He has the very same power with Sensei, and is considered to be the Sensei himself being in another timeline, in which the Kivotos had fallen for some reasons. The "Phrenapates" is, according to Shiroko (Terror, from that timeline), dead and was transformed by the Iridescence.
