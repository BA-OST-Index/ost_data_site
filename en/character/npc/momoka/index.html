Momoka (モモカ) is an NPC in the game.

Description

Momoka is the chief of the General Student Council's Transportation Office. She appears in the Tutorial and some other stories. She has a lazy personality, prefers to avoiding things, and likes eating the chips all the time. Under the current situation, her main task is to distribute tasks to Sensei.
