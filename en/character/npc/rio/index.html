Related Characters

Rio (リオ) is an NPC in the game.

Description

Rio is the former president of Seminar of Millennium Science School, who resigned after the incidents happened in Volume 2. She is the creator of the Eridu City, a fortress city designed to prevent crisis against Kivotos.
