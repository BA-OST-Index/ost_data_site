PMC CEO (PMC CEO) is an NPC in the game.

Description

The CEO of the PMC cooperation and its subsidiaries, one of the main villain in Blue Archive. He appeared in the 1st and Final volumes. His main goal is to bring Kivotos under his rules. He had cooperated with the Black Suit before.
