Kuzunoha (クズノハ) is an NPC in the game.

Description

Kuzunoha is a prophet from Hyakkaryouran Conflict Resolution Council club, who possesses a similar ability with Seia. In the Volume Final, Kuzunoha helped Seia deprive her ability in order to for her to stay safe during the coming incident. She left a message to Sensei stating that there's no cure for Chroma-eroded person and invited Sensei to come to visit her for more details.
