Beatrice (ベアトリーチェ) is an NPC in the game.

Description

Beatrice is an adult of Gematria, who previously the president of Arius Branch School. She's overall a malicious adult and misguided Arius students. She once tried to sacrifice Atsuko and gave away Kivotos' location to Chroma in order to gain power from it, but then failed and directly caused the incidents of Volume Final. She got removed from Gematria due to her action leaking the Kivotos' location, which was seen as a betrayal to the organization.
