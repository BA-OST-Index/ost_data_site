Shiroko (Terror) (シロコ＊テラー) is an NPC in the game.

Description

Shiroko (Terror) is a student appearing in Volume Final. She's essentially an alternative version of Shiroko, but interacted with Chroma and became a Nameless Goddess. She has close relationships with Phrenapates, who seemed to be the Sensei of her own.
