Shinon (シノン) is an NPC in the game.

Description

Shinon is a member of Kronos School's News Club, who is responsible for reporting most news events in Kivotos. She appears with Mai sometimes, and plays a role of live news commentator. She can be seen reporting news energetically on some big news screens.
