Prologue 1-1 is the th story of Chapter 1 of story All Other Stories.

Descriptions:

  • Sensei wakes from a bizarre dream and learns from Nanagami Rin that the General Student Council president is missing. The Academy City, Kivotos, is in chaos. Sensei heads to Schale to try to resolve the situation.

Arguments In the Reception Room

Description: In the reception room, Yuuka from Millennium, Hasumi and Suzumi from Trinity, as well as Chinatsu from Gehenna, were all demanding an excuse for all the chaos after the disappearance of the General Student Council's President. Rin confirmed the news of disappearance, and Sensei greeted everyone. Rin introduced Sensei to everyone, and asked them to escort Sensei to Schale. On the way, they were attacked and the three started to engage, with Sensei commanding in the back.
