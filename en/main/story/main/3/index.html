All Stories of Eden Treaty

Gehenna and Trinity academies are sworn enemies. Will they ever see eye to eye?

  • Chapter 1: Make-Up Work Club, Here We Go!

  • Chapter 2: Paradise Paradox

    The time to sign the Eden Treaty has finally come, and little by little, small cracks begin to form in everyone's daily lives. Each story begins to converge as catastrophe looms.
