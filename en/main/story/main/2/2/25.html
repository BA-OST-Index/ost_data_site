Millennium Science School is the 25th story of Chapter 2, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • The situation is finally resolved. Rio vanishes, the C&C welcomes a new member, and the Game Development Department resumes development of their new project. Peace returns to Millennium.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.
