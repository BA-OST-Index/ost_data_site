Daily Life Destroyed is the 8th story of Chapter 2, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • While tending to an unconscious Momoi, Sensei is contacted by Midori and heads to the Game Development Department where Aris has shut herself in. In spite of Sensei's placating words, Aris reproaches herself for putting Momoi in danger. At that moment, an unexpected visitor arrives with an unsettling revelation.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.
