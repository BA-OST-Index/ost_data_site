A Day at the Game Dev Department is the 2nd story of Chapter 2, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • Sensei pays the Game Development Department a visit for the first time in a while. The members are fielding ideas for the development of their next project, but get distracted watching Aris playing a video game. Aris struggles mid-game upon encountering a cheating player, but gains a dramatic victory thanks to Yuzu's exhilarating performance. However, they fail to make any progress on plans for their next project, and decide to try again tomorrow.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.
