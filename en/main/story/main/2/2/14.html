The Fortress City of Eridu is the 14th story of Chapter 2, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • Contrary to Toki's prediction that Neru would try to rescue Aris on her own, she is faced with the whole of C&C in a ferocious battle. Meanwhile, the Game Development Department blasts their way through every AMAS one by one while making their way to the tower where Aris is located.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.

Story Parts

C&C's Resistance

Description: Toki said she had already known the plan and advised them not to resist anyone. But C&C pretended not understanding it and continued attacking. Between the gaps, Toki explained their operation plan arrogantly, thinking that Neru would be with Sensei; but it was far away from any fact.
