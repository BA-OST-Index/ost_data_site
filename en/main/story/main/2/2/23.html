Pavane for a Princess 2 is the 23th story of Chapter 2, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • The Game Development Department successfully restrain Key with Yuuka and Noa's assistance. As the Game Development Department and Veritas fend off the enemies that are crawling out of the woodwork, Himari helps Momoi, Midori, Yuzu, and Sensei enter Aris' headspace to try to wake her.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.

Story Parts

Precise Timing

Description: Rio explained that Yuuka didn't feel good after the conversation and decided to intervene and help. Yuuka scolded Rio, saying that she would be held accountable severely. The Engineering Department was ready at then, bringing the modified Avant Guard to join in the battle. Key restarted system and began to re-secure resources, gathering troops to the central tower; but they were interfered by Avant Guard and the Engineering Department.

Himari's Anticipation

Description: When Key still thought she had a chance, Himari appeared suddenly. She said that C&C and Eimi had long been blocking the entrance, and there would be no chance for Key to assemble troops in the tower. Rio was surprised that Himari hadn't escaped, and Himari said she had already anticipated such events.

The Way To Wake Up

Description: Himari made it clear that under current situation, it is possible that Key would eventually take over Aris' personality. Therefore, the best solution now should be waking Aris up. When asked how, Himari purposed that the GDD and Sensei shall dive into the machine's consciousness and databases, and bring Aris back at there. Rio pointed out the potential dangers in it, but no one was backing away. Under the protection of almost all effectives from Millennium, Himari started creating a tunnel.
