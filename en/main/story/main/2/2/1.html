A Secret Meeting is the 1st story of Chapter 2, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • Himari visits Rio, the Seminar president. The two discuss the incident that happened between the Game Development Department and C&C and begin to discuss Aris' identity. The meeting turns into a disaster when the two are unable to bridge the gap between their opinions on Aris. However, things take a turn for the worse when Himari is suddenly attacked.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.
