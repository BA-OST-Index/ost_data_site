Guided by Aris 1 is the 4th story of Chapter 2, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • While wandering around campus, Aris runs into Sumire, who is out on a jog. The two of them bond over being both comrades in arms as well as sports. As Aris continues her adventure, she runs into Asuna and Karin in their school uniforms off campus, and she invites them to join her party. That moment, Aris learns that Neru is looking for her and quickly takes off to another location.
