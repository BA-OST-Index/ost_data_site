Guided by Aris 2 is the 5th story of Chapter 2, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • Aris meets with Millennium students who cheer her on, and discusses the Sword of Light with members of the Engineering Department. She enjoys every moment of it. Afterwards, Aris runs into Neru and they visit an arcade to enjoy some video games together. Though they were once enemies against each other, Aris is now proud to declare Neru as her party member.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.
