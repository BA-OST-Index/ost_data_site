Gamers and Friends is the 20th story of Chapter 2, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • The situation seems hopeless and everyone's morale has hit rock bottom. Yuzu, who had been carefully observing the battle between Neru and Toki, proposes a way to neutralize Abi-Eshuh.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.

Story Parts

1. A Sense of Loss

All retreated into a closer building. Chihiro summarized the current situation: the C&C were not strong enough (with Neru still in coma), the Engineering Department were exhausted, and the combat power of GDD could not even be counted. Everyone thought believed that every method had been used, and the "bad ending", as Midori suggested, had arrived.

2. Still Got A Chance

Hearing "Bad Ending", Neru suddenly woke up. Neru steadied herself and demanded an answer from GDD, "Is that all you got to rescue Aris?" Though serverly injured, she said she was on the same boat with others, and should not waste this opportunity. When Momoi asked how to actually make it, Yuzu reported her discovery.

3. Cheaters & Tricks

Yuzu was convinced that Neru did inflict damage on Toki. Chihiro pointed out the Toki's suit didn't seem to be functioning well when calculating the parameters of both the fall and the attack. Yuzu asked whether Neru was at her best in close combat; Neru replied yes, and Yuzu then formed a plan - with the usage of the "tricks."
