Turning the Tides is the 17th story of Chapter 2, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • With Sumire's help, Chihiro secures the Mirror from the contraband storage and succeeds in taking control of Eridu's network, and the Game Development Department seizes this moment to launch a counterattack.
  • The Game Development Department succeed in defeating the Avant Guard with the help of the Engineering Department, but the aftermath causes the Engineering Department to retire from combat. However, they continue cheering on the Game Development Department's forward charge.

Battle/Bond Track Info

Story Parts

Timely Help

Description: Chihiro's appearance made everyone very excited. Others at Veritas also connected to the comm. Hare was very curious about how Chihiro did it, and Chihiro said lightly that it was the "Mirror" came in handy. Everyone remembered the confrontation between Yuzu and Neru at that time, and was amazed by the plan. Momoi questioned the means of retrieving it, and Sumire happened to connect to the comm. Despite the successful hacking, the firewall started making the communication unstable. Veritas promised that the communication would not be interrupted, and everyone continued to combat the Avant Guard.
