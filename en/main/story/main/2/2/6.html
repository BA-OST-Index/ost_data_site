A Strange Machine 1 is the 6th story of Chapter 2, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • One day, Veritas reaches out to Sensei about the discovery of a suspicious machine. On the way to their club room, Sensei joins up with the Game Development Department to investigate the machine, but none of them are able to get to the bottom of its true nature... As if suddenly drawn by an inexplicable force, Aris touches the machine and activates it.
