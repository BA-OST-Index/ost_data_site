Checkmate is the 16th story of Chapter 2, Volume 2 of the main story (min favor rank ).

Descriptions:

  • Rio's victory is sealed in front of Himari, but Himari succeeds in escaping with Eimi's help. Meanwhile, cut off from Veritas's support, the Game Development Department find themselves with their backs against the wall until they are dramatically rescued by Chihiro, the vice president of Veritas.

Battle/Bond Track Info
