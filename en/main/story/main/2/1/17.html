Just Getting Warmed Up is the 17th story of Chapter 1, Volume 2 of the main story (min favor rank ).

Descriptions:

  • Akane apologizes to Neru for her failures, but Neru is more interested in how Aris managed to go toe-to-toe with the Maid Club. Back at the club room, the Game Development Department tries to use the Mirror to decrypt G.BIBLE.
