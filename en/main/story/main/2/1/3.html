To The Ruins is the 3rd story of Chapter 1, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • Midori and Momoi are searching the ruins for G.BIBLE, the holy grail of game development. Unfortunately, hostile robots force them to retreat to the abandoned factory.
