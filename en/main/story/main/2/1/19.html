Double O is the 19th story of Chapter 1, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • The Game Development Department submits Tales Saga Chronicle 2 just before their deadline. Right after it uploads, the club room suddenly becomes the target of a shootout.
  • The shooter was Neru, the president of the Maid Club. Claiming that her last battle with Aris had piqued her interest, Neru challenges Aris to a fight. One destructive battle later, and Neru, satisfied, goes on her way.

1. Finishing The Project

The GDD finally finished it at the very last moment. Momoi uploaded the demo for everyone to download. The comments started flooding in, as the download counter counting fast. When everyone was worried and nervous, the club room was hit by a few bullet shots. Everyone began to engage.
