Nerds vs. Maids is the 13th story of Chapter 1, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • While Aris and Veritas create a distraction, Midori, Momoi, and Sensei attempt a frontal assault on Seminar.
  • Veritas' hacking ends up trapping Akane in a sealed room.
