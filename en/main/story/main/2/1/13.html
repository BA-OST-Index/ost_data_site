Nerds vs. Maids is the 13th story of Chapter 1, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • While Aris and Veritas create a distraction, Midori, Momoi, and Sensei attempt a frontal assault on Seminar.
  • Veritas' hacking ends up trapping Akane in a sealed room.

Yet both the lifts and the doors on the top floor should only be verified by fingerprints, and failed to verify that would also trigger multiple mechanisms. In order to break through the security system, Hibiki proposed to disable the system with EMP for a while, and by that means the Veritas would be able to hack in.
