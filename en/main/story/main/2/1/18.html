G.BIBLE is the 18th story of Chapter 1, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • As it turns out, G.BIBLE only contains a simple maxim: "Love video games." Their sky-high expectations dashed, the Game Development Department, with the help of Aris, begin development on Tales Saga Chronicle 2.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.

3. The Aftermath of TSC

Yuzu recalled the flooding negative comments in the days after the release of TSC. Her self-confidence was greatly damaged. The door was knocked at then, and initially Yuzu thought it was an angry player, but didn't expect to be Momoi and Midori. Both not only spoke highly of TSC, but would also become the members of the GDD afterwards.
