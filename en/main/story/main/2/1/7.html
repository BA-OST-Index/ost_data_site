Much Too Big to Be Called a Gun is the 7th story of Chapter 1, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • The Game Development Department heads to the Engineering Department to get Aris a firearm. Aris ends up picking a railgun meant to be attached to an intergalactic battleship. The Engineering Department challenges Aris to a fight to test her abilities.
  • Aris skillfully uses the railgun to fend off the Engineering Department members, despite never having held such a weapon before. Utaha, the president of the Engineering Department, begins to become suspicious of Aris' unusual physical prowess.

2. Visiting Engineering Department

Utaha and others welcomed them, and Hibiki took the initiative to choose weapons for Aris. But Aris soon put her attention onto a railgun, which was then introduced by Kotori. She mentioned that the development was terminated due to funding issues. All of the Engineering Department thought Aris couldn't lift it up, but Aris had not only lifted it, but also shot a hole on the club room; as a last resort, the Engineering Department challenged the Game Development Department, that they could take it away if they won them.
