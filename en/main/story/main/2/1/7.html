Much Too Big to Be Called a Gun is the 7th story of Chapter 1, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • 游戏开发部学生为了取得能让爱丽丝使用的枪支,便前往 千年学园的<工程师部>。爱丽丝在那里选择了宇宙战舰用的轨道炮。工程师部为了测试她的能力而向她挑战。
  • Aris skillfully uses the railgun to fend off the Engineering Department members, despite never having held such a weapon before. Utaha, the president of the Engineering Department, begins to become suspicious of Aris' unusual physical prowess.
