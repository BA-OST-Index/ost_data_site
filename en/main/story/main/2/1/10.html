1. Outside the Factory

Outside the factory, everyone was met with fierce attack from the robots. Midori wanted to retreat but was rejected by Momoi. She thought that the robots would eventually gather and made the situation far worse than now. Encouraged by Aris and Momoi, everyone decided to engage.
