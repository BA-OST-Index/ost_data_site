Yuuka's Questions is the 9th story of Chapter 1, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • Satisfied with Aris' answers, Yuuka admits Aris as an official member of the club, but remains adamant about shutting down the Game Development Department if their next game doesn't come together. Once again, the club members head to the ruins in search of G.BIBLE.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.

Story Parts

1. Are You Being Kidnapped

Yuuka first whispered to ask whether she, Aris, was being kidnapped or not, but was cut off by Momoi and Midori. Yuuka had to restarted the questioning.
