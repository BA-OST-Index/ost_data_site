A Spreading Danger is the 15th story of Chapter 1, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • Having taken too long, Akane and Karin escape their bonds, an event spelling disaster for the Game Development Department's mission. Just then, Aris comes to the rescue and succeeds in infiltrating the Student Council's contraband storage room.
