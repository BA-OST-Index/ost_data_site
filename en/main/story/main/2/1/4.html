On That Day, We Met a Girl is the 4th story of Chapter 1, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • In the abandoned factory, the Game Development Department finds a humanoid robot by the name of Aris. For the sake of the club, Momoi brings Aris back to their club room to induct her as a new member.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.

Story Parts

1. In The Factory

The three ran into the factory and found no robots continuing chasing them. Right at the moment, the facility's built-in system began identifying them and recognized Sensei, granting them permission to proceed. They dropped to the bottom thanks to the door opened at the floor.

3. Meeting Aris

In one sinkhole, the three found Aris who was asleep, as well as some text like "AL-1S". Momoi took pity on her and decided to take her back to Millennium. She also gave her own clothes for Aris to wear on. Aris woke up right after the interaction with Sensei.
