I, Aris... is the 5th story of Chapter 1, Volume 2 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • Aris plays and thoroughly enjoys the Game Development Department's previous title, Tales Saga Chronicle. The club members are touched by her glowing response.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.

Story Parts

1. Discussion

Momoi and Midori discussed the feasibility of disguising Aris as a Millennium student. Momoi was in charge of getting a falsified student ID card, while Midori and Yuzu would teach Aris how to "speak", in the hope of modifying her fixed speech pattern.

2. Learning How To Speak

Momoi left in advance and Midori was troubled with how to teach. At this moment Aris found a magazine about the game they made before. Midori then decided to let Aris try their games, attempting to teach her with in-game dialogues.

3. Game Start

Aris started the game while Momoi and Midori explained about the game. Though faced with countless failures and crashed her system for several times, she kept going on and eventually made it.

4. Ending

With the help of Momoi and Midori, Aris was able to reach the True End within 3 hours. When asked about the comments on the game, Aris thought it was fun. Hearing this, Yuzu stepped out of the wardrobe, thanking Aris sincerely.
