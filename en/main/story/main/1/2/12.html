Secrets is the 12th story of Chapter 2, Volume 1 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • After sending her enraged underclassmen home, Hoshino reveals to Sensei the secret deal she was offered by the man in the black suit. Sensei tries to console her, but the next day finds no trace of Hoshino, save for her resignation letter.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.

Story Parts

1. Memory

Hoshino remembered the days with that former Student Union president. That president held a positive view of the future and believed that the future of Abydos would not be just like that. She also gifted a poster of the old festival to Hoshino. But Hoshino cut her down and denied her, ripping apart the poster at hand.

2. Reviewing the Situation

Everyone still couldn't understand what PMC was looking for; after all, high value stuff like oils had long been proved to be non-existed. Shiroko said she wanted to investigate it first, but got Ayane caught to be having the previous idea. Hoshino advised everyone to calm down and returned to take rests first.

3. A Talk With Hoshino

Shiroko and Sensei waited into the nights. Hoshino sent Shiroko away for "being too tired". Sensei remained in the classroom and took out Hoshino's resignation application. Hoshino tried to move the problem onto Shiroko, but Sensei didn't let go. Hoshino agreed to talk about the situation, but first she would like to be out at the corridors.

6. Hiring Professionals

Hoshino suspected that PMC (especially the Black Suit) seemed to be hiring some professionals. Hoshino said that she had never seen people like him in Kivotos, and it appeared to her that even Kaiser Director feared him as well. As for the resignation application, Hoshino said it was nothing serious and ripped it apart in front of Sensei. Hoshino said she would made it clear for everyone, but also felt that it was the only option to accept that deal. Before leaving, Sensei made a promise to Hoshino that he would eventually come up with solutions.
