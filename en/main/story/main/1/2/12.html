Secrets is the 12th story of Chapter 2, Volume 1 of the main story (min favor rank ).

Descriptions:

  • After sending her enraged underclassmen home, Hoshino reveals to Sensei the secret deal she was offered by the man in the black suit. Sensei tries to console her, but the next day finds no trace of Hoshino, save for her resignation letter.

Story Parts

1. Memory

  • Hoshino remembers the days with that former Student Union president. That president held a positive view of the future and believed that the future of Abydos would not be just like that. She also gifted a poster of the old festival to Hoshino. But Hoshino interrupted her and denied her, ripping apart the poster at hand.

  • Everyone still can't understand what PMC is looking for; after all, high value stuff like oils has long been proved to be non-existed. 3. A Talk With Hoshino

  • Shiroko and Sensei waits into the night. Hoshino sends Shiroko away for "being too tired". Sensei remains in the classroom and takes out Hoshino's resignation application. Hoshino tries to move the problem onto Shiroko, but Sensei didn't let go. Hoshino agrees to talk about the situation, but first she would like to be out at the corridors.

6. PMC Hiring Professionals

  • Hoshino suspects that PMC (especially the Black Suit) seems to be hiring some professionals. Hoshino says that she has never seen anyone like him in Kivotos, and it appears to her that even Kaiser Director fears him as well. As for the resignation application, Hoshino says it is nothing serious and rips it apart in front of Sensei. Hoshino says she'll made it clear for everyone, but also feels that it is the only option to accept that deal. Before leaving, Sensei makes a promise to Hoshino that he will eventually come up with solutions.
