The Abydos Siege Part 2 is the 15th story of Chapter 2, Volume 1 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • Despite the Kaiser director's outrage, Sensei, the Foreclosure Task Force, and Problem Solver make use of the sudden turn of events to launch a counteroffensive against the PMC forces.
  • The PMC forces finally retreats following an arduous battle. Later, Sensei seeks out Black Suit personally to negotiate Hoshino's return.

Battle/Bond Track Info

  • OST 18. Mechanical JUNGLE  by KARUT

Story Parts

1. Betraying PMC

  • The director is both shocked and angry to see that the Problem Solver 68 betrayed them, but the latter insults them back. Sensei confronts the director, demanding that Hoshino be returned. The director doesn't step back. Everyone is forced to engage the enemies.
