Everyone Has a Story Part 1 is the 4th story of Chapter 2, Volume 1 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • Back at the club room, everyone goes over what had happened. Ayane and Serika do some digging into administrative documents and tell the group that the majority of the Abydos district's land has been transfered to the Kaiser Corporation.

Story Parts

1. Meeting Nonomi

  • Sensei meets Nonomi at the school gate and Nonomi asks the physical conditions of Master Shiba. Sensei says he is in good conditions, but Nonomi guesses it right that there might be some other problems. Nonomi purposes to go back to the classroom and expresses her appreciation for Sensei's help these days. Nonomi hopes that Sensei could continue going with them and Sensei assures.

3. An Argument

  • Shiroko seems to be questioning Hoshino seriously, but Hoshino pretends to know nothing. Nonomi and Sensei break into the room, yet none of them mentions anything - only "something with Hoshino". Nonomi says there's nothing should be hid between the Task Force, and Hoshino is forced to admit that it was only "Shiroko scolding her for sleeping too much". Despite that, Nonomi senses dishonest in it.

4. An Emergency

  • Serika and Ayane has obtained a map of land ownership throughout Abydos and confirmed an emergency. Initially, most of them, including Hoshino, thought that Abydos is obviously owned by the school; yet based on the transaction records, the most of the land has long been owned by the Kaiser Group.
