What Slipped Through the Cracks is the 13th story of Chapter 2, Volume 1 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • In her letter, Hoshino reveals that she joined Kaiser PMC in exchange for Abydos High School's debt being forgiven. Now, without Hoshino, the remaining members of the Foreclosure Task Force scramble when PMC forces close in on Abydos High School.

Battle/Bond Track Info

  • OST  20. Tech N Tech

Story Parts

1. Hoshino's Resignation Application

Hoshino left an resignation application and a letter. In the letter, Hoshino confessed the fact that she was going to be a mercenary at PMC, and said that "(the Black Suit) would take on half of Abydos' debt".
