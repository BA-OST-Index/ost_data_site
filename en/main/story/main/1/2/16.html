Battle Between Adults is the 16th story of Chapter 2, Volume 1 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • Black Suit attempts to coerce [USERNAME] Sensei into giving Hoshino up for his experiments, but Sensei makes it perfectly clear that Hoshino will be rescued—no matter what.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.

Story Parts

1. The People Outside Kivotos

  • The Black Suit says he knows quite well about Sensei. In Black Suit's eyes, Sensei is the biggest obstacle and so he doesn't and shouldn't be hostile with Sensei, but rather he should seek cooperation. Sensei asks about his identity, and Black Suit says he is a member of "Gematria". He then asks what Sensei is looking for in Kivotos, but Sensei doesn't listen. The Black Suit adds that, Sensei's behaviour is no longer justified at this time, and he asks whether Sensei even knows well about his rights. He says that Hoshino is no longer a student belonging to Abydos, and mentions about the resignation letter.

2. I Did Not Sign It

  • It turns out there is a column named "Advisor" on the application. And as a teacher invited from Schale, the application is nothing more than a piece of paper if without Sensei's signature. And so Hoshino still belongs to the Foreclosure Task Force, still belongs to Abydos High School, and of course still one of Sensei's students.

3. Sensei's Criticism

  • The Black Suit gets the idea and disdains such troublesome procedures. Sensei then goes on criticizing the Black Suits, saying they "not only deceive those childrens, tramp their hearts, but also take advantages of their sufferings." The Black Suit doesn't deny the fact that he pursues self-interests, but that's with a clear boundary: the desertification of Abydos isn't their doings, and they are just taking advantages of this disaster.

4. The Truth About The World

  • "Those with power exploit those without, those with knowledge exploit those without," Black Suit explains the truth about the world to Sensei. The Black Suit repeats that deal, wanting Sensei to stop interfering them. They promises they will take on half of the debt, and they can keep others at Abydos High School safe and sound. But Sensei rejects it still.

5. Adult's Card

  • The Black Suit cannot comprehend Sensei's choice. He doubts about the ability that Sensei possesses to be enemy with them, and Sensei takes out the Adult's Card - a weapon exclusive to Sensei. But the Black Suit warns Sensei, that the more he use it, the more it will be destroyed, and it will also cost Sensei's life and time. The Black Suit reminds Sensei that he still need to do a lot of daily stuff with it, and mentions again that this has nothing to do with him in the very beginning. Yet Sensei rejects that again.

6. Adult's Responsibility

  • The Black Suit cannot understand Sensei's choice. He asks, questions Sensei, while Sensei just says, "For the sufferings of those children, no one's willing to take the responsibility." The Black Suit is confused, saying that Sensei has neither any kinship nor consanguinity with them, but only an outsider who happened to meet them - and none of the responsibilities should be shouldered by Sensei. Sensei then says, "That's what an adult should do."

7. An Incorrect Thought

  • The Black Suit now understands and begins to correct Sensei's ideas. "The so-called adults, are the ones who are able to transform the society to one that fits well in their own ideal dream, the ones who make rules, define what's normal or not, who's ordinary or not. The ones who use authority, knowledge and power to govern those without - and that's what I call an adult." The Black Suit stresses that it isn't unrelated to Sensei, as Sensei could have become the ruler of the Kivotos, yet Sensei doesn't. The Black Suit can't get Sensei's intent.
