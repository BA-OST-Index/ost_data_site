Everyone Has a Story Part 2 is the 5th story of Chapter 2, Volume 1 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • Hoshino recounts history to the Foreclosure Task Force. It's likely the now-defunct Abydos Student Council tried to repay its debts until the land was eventually repossessed by the Kaiser Corporation. Sensei suggests following Hina's hint and to go to the Abydos Desert.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.

Story Parts

1. Kaiser Group

  • The truth is, most of the land in Abydos has been owned by Kaiser Corporation (Kaiser Construction to be exact). Not only is everyone confused about the reason behind it, but also how it was done. Hoshino and Ayane says that these transactions should only be made by the people from the former student union, as they had the highest authority.

2. Student Union At The Time

  • Hoshino describes the situation back then, and in a nutshell, it was in chaos. The desertification was extremely serious at then, and the school was forced to relocate for several times. Not only did the student number dropping fast, teachers nowhere to find, but also most of the student union had resigned. There was only one left, but that person was unreliable, just like Hoshino had a bad temper at then, according to Hoshino. Everyone is shocked by the fact. Shiroko says Hoshino has no need to belittle herself, saying that she is a responsible president.

3. Digging Into The Reason

  • Everyone starts to discuss why the school would have decided to sell the land to Kaiser. Everyone thinks it is because of the debts. However, due to the low selling prices, it was no use after all. Sensei suddenly notices that, both the lender and buyer are all Kaiser, and Abydos is in fact in a trap, whose goal might actually be the land of Abydos. Serika can't understand why the student union would do such things, but Hoshino says that people would certainly do such if they are cornered.

4. A Clear Situation

  • With current clues on hand, Ayane finally confirms that Kaiser's goal is exactly the land of Abydos. The exact reason remains unknown. Sensei tells everyone the things he heard from Hina, and everyone decides to go to the desert immediately.

5. Hoshino's Secret

  • Before leaving, Shiroko stops Sensei. In a classroom, Shiroko shows Sensei the thing she found in Hoshino's bag: a resignation application, with the name "Takanashi Hoshino". Sensei asks why Shiroko would do so, and Shiroko says it was because she thought that recently, Hoshino seems to be behaving particularly strange, and there must be something hidden.
