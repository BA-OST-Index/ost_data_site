Farewell, Problem Solver 68 is the 3rd story of Chapter 2, Volume 1 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • Sensei sees Problem Solver out of Abydos. In the mean time, Ayane and Serika pay Master Shiba a visit in the hospital. He tells the girls that the ramen shop's building has been taken over by the Kaiser Corporation.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.

Story Parts

1. Problem Solver On Move

  • Due to the inability to pay rent, as well as exposing their location during the fight with the Prefect Team, the Problem Solver has to move their office out of the place. Though saying "Because this is how a badass should be...", Aru is interrupted and commented with "really worries others".

2. Meeting Problem Solver

  • Sensei meets all the Problem Solver 68 on move and wishes them a happy trip. Aru says it's a pleasant experience to be a cooperation partner and promises they will come back to Abydos for him, and especially for the ramen. The truck loaded with all the stuff disappears at the cross then.

3. A Conversation With Master Shiba

  • Ayane and Serika visits Master Shiba and expresses their pity on the destruction of the ramen shop. But unexpectedly, Master Shiba says that he has already received a notice to close down the store, which makes the two very surprised. Master Shiba explains that due to the inability to pay the debts, the lands of Abydos are not belong to the school and are bought by Kaiser. Master Shiba also mentions that there is a big bag of money in front of the store, and Sensei suggests that they might be used as a reconstruction fund.
