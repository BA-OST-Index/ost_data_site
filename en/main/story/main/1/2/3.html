Farewell, Problem Solver 68 is the 3rd story of Chapter 2, Volume 1 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • Sensei sees Problem Solver out of Abydos. In the mean time, Ayane and Serika pay Master Shiba a visit in the hospital. He tells the girls that the ramen shop's building has been taken over by the Kaiser Corporation.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.

Problem Solver On Move

Description: Due to the inability to pay rent, as well as exposing their location during the fight with the Prefect Team, the Problem Solver had to move their office out of the place. Though saying "Because this is how a badass should be...", Aru was interrupted and was commented with "really worries others".

A Conversation With Master Shiba

Description: Ayane and Serika visited Master Shiba and expressed their pity on the destruction of the ramen shop. But unexpectedly, Master Shiba said that he had already received a notice to close down the store, which made the two very surprised. Master Shiba explained that due to the inability to pay the debts, the lands of Abydos were not belong to the school and was bought by Kaiser. Master Shiba also mentioned that there was a big bag of money in front of the store, and Sensei suggested that they might be used as a reconstruction fund.
