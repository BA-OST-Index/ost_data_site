Story Parts

1. Gehenna Academy

  • Sensei comes to Gehenna and hopes to have a talk with Hina, but gets intercepted by Iori. Iori says it is impossible, unless Sensei's willing to lick her feet - and Sensei does it without a second thought. Iori curses at Sensei and they happens to meet Hina. Seeing Sensei kneeling down, Hina asks Sensei to rise. For some reasons, Iori mentions about the licking stuff and Hina is in great shock immediately.
