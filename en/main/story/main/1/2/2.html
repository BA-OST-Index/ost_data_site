The Gehenna Head Prefect

The Gehenna Head Prefect is the 2nd story of Chapter 2, Volume 1 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • Having heard Ako's story, Hina promptly apologizes for the Prefect Team overstepping their bounds. While commanding her forces to pull out, Hina subtly informs Sensei that the Kaiser Corporation is up to something in the Abydos Desert.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.

Story Parts

1. Hard to Explain

  • Ako tries to explain that she is fighting against Problem Solver 68, but the latter has been long gone. Hina gets even angrier but then expresses her understanding, yet she still points out that such political issues should be left to the Pandemonium Society. Hina says Ako will be punished with confinement.

2. Negotiation With Hina

  • Hina says the conflict is indeed their wrong doings, but it's also true that they had interfered with Gehenna's official duties. The Abydos remain hostile, though. At this moment Ayane mentions Hoshino, and Hoshino appears just on time. Hina exchanges a few words with Hoshino and expresses her surprise at Hoshino's dramatic character change. Hina mentions "that incident" and suddenly realizes something.
