Kaiser PMC Part 2 is the 10th story of Chapter 2, Volume 1 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • A supposed Kaiser board member appears before the Foreclosure Task Force and introduces themselves as the creditor of Abydos.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.

Story Parts

1. Not Near Ending

  • The PMC is too strong for everyone to escape. The director of PMC suddenly visits at this time, and Hoshino recognizes him and remembers something.

3. Discussing the Debt

  • The PMC director proposes to add the losses caused by everyone to the amount owed. Hoshino recognizes him, and the latter says hello to Hoshino. Then he "accidentally" leaks his hiring of the Problem Solver 68 and the Helmet Group. Afterwards, he says he wants to have a good talk with Abydos about the debt.
