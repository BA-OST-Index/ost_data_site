The Abydos Siege Part 1 is the 14th story of Chapter 2, Volume 1 of the main story (min favor rank ).

Descriptions:

  • After joining up with Kaiser PMC, Hoshino defects when she learns that Abydos High School is under attack, but is then arrested by Black Suit. When all hope seems lost, Problem Solver 68 returns to Abydos to lend their aid to the Foreclosure Task Force.

Story Parts

1. Kaiser Director

  • Kaiser Director arrives and everyone is immediately triggered. Though claiming that they would be reported to the General Student Council, the director says it is useless and the time just proves that no one could be helping them. The director even points out that, due to Hoshino's dropout, Abydos High School is no longer existed. Everyone is shocked, but still argues with the existence of "The Foreclosure Task Force" - however, according to Ayane, since it is established after the dissolution of the Student Union, the Task Force is in fact a club that hasn't recognized officially. As a result, neither can it represent the school, nor can it be proved to be existed.

2. Hoshino

  • Hoshino is in great shock upon hearing PMC were attacking Abydos. The Black Suit explains that, after her dropout, Abydos will no longer have a student belonging to the Student Union, and therefore the school will not exist. The Black suit expresses his interest on establishing a new school purposed by PMC, but then clarifies that their goal is actually Hoshino herself. The fact of cooperating with Kaiser is simply because the interests aren't in conflict. The Black Suit says, as the highest "Mythic" in Kivotos, he will be carrying out experiments on her.

3. Hoshino's Regrets

  • Hanging on the experiment platform, Hoshino keeps her head low, resenting her own innocence. She feels sorry to everyone in the Foreclosure Task Force, as well as the former member Yume. Once again, she is deceived by adults.

5. Problem Solver 68

  • Problem Solver 68 arrives upon hearing the explosions, acknowledging that the PMC was really out of line. Aru slams the Task Force for "giving up when your friends were in crisis", and repeats the motto that once the Masked Swimsuit Gang told her. In return for the help Abydos had made, Problem Solver 68 join in the battle.
