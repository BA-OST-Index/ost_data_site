To the Black Market! Part 2 is the 12th story of Chapter 1, Volume 1 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • Hifumi agrees to guide The Foreclosure Task Force around the market in return for helping her earlier. Meanwhile, the boss of Problem Solver 68, Aru, struggles to get a loan so she can pay off her earlier mistake.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.
