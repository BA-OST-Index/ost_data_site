You'll Pay for This! is the 19th story of Chapter 1, Volume 1 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • Unaware of Aru's true feelings, the Foreclosure Task Force blame Aru for the heinous atrocity she accidentally caused. Eventually, Aru ends up confronting the team, pretending to be the villain they believe she is.
  • During the climax of the battle between Problem Solver and Foreclosure Task Force, the combatants are suddenly shelled from afar. Ayane announces that the cannons were fired by Gehenna Academy's Prefect Team.
