Rescue Mission! is the 6th story of Chapter 1, Volume 1 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • When Serika goes incommunicado after parting at the ramen shop, Sensei uses Schale's authority to triangulate the position of Serika's cell phone and sets out to rescue her.
