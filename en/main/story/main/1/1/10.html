In Search of a Clue is the 10th story of Chapter 1, Volume 1 of the main story (min favor rank ).

Descriptions:

  • The Foreclosure Task Force learn that Problem Solver 68 is a group of delinquents from Gehenna Academy and that the Helmet Gang's weapons came from the Black Market. Sensei and the students head there to investigate.

Story Parts

1. Meeting Ayane and Mutsuki

  • On the way to school, Sensei meets Ayane. Ayane says she is going to prepare for the debt payment at school and has found everything about the Problem Solver 68. It is then that Mutsuki appears. Mutsuki invites Sensei to pay a visit on them, but is scolded by Ayane before leaving.

3. About The Problem Solver 68

  • Ayane finds out all the information of Problem Solver 68, stressing that they are seen as problem children in Gehenna, and therefore they should be handled with extra caution. On the other hand, Ayane thinks that if they need to find the origin of the illegal weapons, they might have to visit the Black Market.
