A Day in the Life of Serika is the 5th story of Chapter 1, Volume 1 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • Sensei and the Foreclosure Task Force trail Serika to her part-time job at a ramen shop. They end up sharing a nice meal with her, but Serika is still quite sullen. On her way back from work, Serika is kidnapped by the Helmet Gang.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.

Story Parts

1. Bumping Into Serika

  • Sensei bumps into Serika and greets her nicely, wanting to know where and what she's going. Serika dodges but was follows by Sensei closely; she later admits that she is going to part-time jobs and askes Sensei not to follow, yet Sensei doesn't listen.

2. The Ramen Store

  • Sensei brings the rest of the Foreclosure Task Force to the very ramen store that Serika is working for. Though surprised, Serika leads the way to the table; later, Nonomi purposes to pay with her cards, but Sensei does that with his own at last. After lunch, everyone returns to school under the strong request by the embarrassed Serika.
