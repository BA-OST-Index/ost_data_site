The Gathering Storm is the 17th story of Chapter 1, Volume 1 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • Aru grapples with the thought of using funds acquired by the Foreclosure Task Force to attack Abydos High School. To lift her spirits, Problem Solver takes her to get Shiba Seki ramen. Meanwhile, a man in a black suit appears before Hoshino with a clandestine proposal.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.
