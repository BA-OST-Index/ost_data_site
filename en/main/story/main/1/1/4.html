The Task Force's Predicament is the 4th story of Chapter 1, Volume 1 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • The students explain to Sensei that their school's over 900 million yen in debt and how difficult it is for them to even keep up with interest payments. When Sensei offers to help, Serika goes off on a tirade about how grown-ups can't be trusted.

2. The Debt

  • From what the Hoshino and Ayane says, Sensei acknowledges the debt worth 962.36 million yen. Sensei also knows that sandstorms keep occurring in recent years, and no efforts are achieved despite the large amount of funds that the school has put into it. As no banks are willing to lend them money, they are forced to turn to loan sharks. The deserialization only goes worse and worse, together with the skyrocketing debt; as a result, many students has left the school.
