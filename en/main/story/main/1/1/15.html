Riding off Into the Sunset! is the 15th story of Chapter 1, Volume 1 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • After outrunning the Black Market Guard, the group begins to question their resolve. Hoshino persuades them to take only the documents they need. Later, Problem Solver discovers the piles of money that were left behind.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.
