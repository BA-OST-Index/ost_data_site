The Masked Swimsuit Gang! Part 2 is the 14th story of Chapter 1, Volume 1 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • Aru is waiting for a loan interview at the Stygian Bank when the masked members of the Foreclosure Task Force burst in and steal all the bank's money. The Black Market Guard gives chase, and Aru admires the students' act of pure villainy.

Battle/Bond Track Info

  • OST  18. Mechanical JUNGLE

Story Parts

1. Aru Being Rejected

After waiting hours long for the loan interview, Aru eventually was rejected for a loan due to poor records and unable to mortgage any property. When arguing with the staff, Abydos broke in.
