To the Black Market! Part 1 is the 11th story of Chapter 1, Volume 1 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • The Foreclosure Task Force bumps into Trinity student Hifumi at the Black Market. She came to buy a limited-edition Peroro plush, but was chased by a group of bullies. The task force steps in, and a fight ensues.
  • Having beat back the bullies, Hifumi and the Foreclosure Task Force leave the scene before an even bigger mess happens.

Battle/Bond Track Info

  • OST  73. Interface (Hard Arrange)

Story Parts

1. First Time At The Market

The Foreclosure Task Force was amazed by the scale of the market. Then everyone met Hifumi, who was being chased by some bullies at that moment, and saved her for good.
