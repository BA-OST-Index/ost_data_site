The Truth Revealed is the 16th story of Chapter 1, Volume 1 of the main story.

Descriptions:

  • The documents reveal the true mastermind: a mega-syndicate known as the Kaiser Corporation. Confronted with such a seemingly insurmountable societal evil, the dejected students of the Foreclosure Task Force call it a day.

Battle/Bond Track Info

This story contains no battle/recollection lobby part, therefore there's no such track information.

1. Truths Surfacing

After a careful inspection on the records, they did find out the money was flowing into the bank, then was directly used to support the Helmet Gang. Everything showed that Kaiser Corporation seemed to be plotting against Abydos.
