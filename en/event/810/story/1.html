Valentine's Day Teamwork Part 1 is the 1st story of the event Schale's Happy ♡ Valentine Patrol.

Descriptions:

  • A festival is held in D.U. in celebration of Valentine's Day. Sensei goes on patrol after the Valkyrie Police School's Fubuki and Kirino request assistance in maintaining the security of the festival.
