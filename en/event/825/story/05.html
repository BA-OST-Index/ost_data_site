Hello There. Hop. is the 5th story of the event Alabaster Calling Card ~Mansion Masquerade and the Essence of Beauty~.

Descriptions:

  • The Game Development Department members are working as maids for the mansion as well as bodyguards. They struggle with their unfamiliar duties, but are helped by Toki, who suddenly shows up dressed as a bunny girl. The members and Sensei are surprised by Toki's sudden appearance. Before they have a chance to greet each other, Benevolent Thief appears in the middle of the party.
