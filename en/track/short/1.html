Watashitachi No Quest (Our Quest) is the No. 1 track in the Short Animation.

The ending song of the short animation "beautiful day dreamer" about the Game Development Department, sung by the fours of the GDD. The song has two versions, one is two-minute long and the other one is four; the former is used at the end of both the animation and the Episode 25, Chapter 2, Volume 2 in-game.
