Irasshaimase is the No. 17 track in the OST.

The 17th track in Blue Archive OST, usually plays in the "Store" page. It's also widely known as the song for the recollection lobby of Yuuka's.
Its title can be translated into "Welcome" in English, the greetings when you enter a store or restaurant.
